Why numbers were “wrong” before
Most mismatches come from (1) selecting the wrong PPR report filter, (2) line item names not matching exactly, or (3) OP rate coming back as 0 when a “Total” line has no plan productivity.

This version fixes that by:
  • Exact matching (no smart/fuzzy) + clear NOT FOUND flags
  • Safe fallbacks for OP rate (if 0 or missing)
  • Separate editable IB and OB mapping tables
Config is saved automatically to localStorage after edits.
